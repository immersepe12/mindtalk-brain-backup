## KEY STRATEGIC FINDING — brand-fragmentation across AI engines

The recurring pattern: **when a Cadabams entity is cited for commercial/condition queries, it is usually the hospital domain (cadabamshospitals.com) or the Google business listing — NOT the mindtalk.in platform.**

- "online therapy bangalore": ChatGPT → **Cadabam's Mindtalk** (business listing) 🟢 BUT Claude → cadabamshospitals.com ❌
- "ocd specialist bangalore": Google AI OV → **mindtalk.in/Doctors** 🟢 BUT ChatGPT → Cadabams Hospitals (group) ❌; Perplexity → no Cadabams at all
- "cadabams mental health": Perplexity → cadabamshospitals.com, never mindtalk.in
- "depression treatment online india": Google organic → cadabamshospitals.com/Depression, not mindtalk.in

This mirrors the digital-presence "brand SERP fragmentation / intra-group cannibalization" finding. mindtalk.in and cadabamshospitals.com compete for the same Cadabams citations; the hospital domain often wins the AI citation. **AEO consolidation (clear entity definition: "Mindtalk = the Cadabams online therapy platform") would help AI engines route commercial citations to mindtalk.in instead of the hospital site.**
